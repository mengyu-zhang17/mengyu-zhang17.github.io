About

Hi there! I am currently a PhD student at UC Irvine, School of Medicine, advised by Professor Vladimir J.Kefalov. My research focuses on Retinitis Pigmentosa (RP) therapy.

I have contributed to impactful projects, including validating molecular mechanisms of water disinfection methods and leading lab initiatives at Lingang Laboratory. In Summer 2023, I interned at Lingang Laboratory, where I developed a Python program for retinal signal analysis and streamlined mouse genotyping protocols.

I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Biological Science from Tsinghua University, with a second major in Economics. During my undergraduate years, I worked on a Student Research Training project under Professor Yun Lu, optimizing DNA extraction methods for environmental pathogens and assessing endotoxin-related lung inflammation risks.

I was fortunate to gain teaching experience as a Teaching Assistant for Engineering Microbiology at Tsinghua in Spring 2022, where I guided students in experimental design and contributed to textbook compilation. My work has been published in journals like Environmental Chemistry Letters and Science of The Total Environment.
